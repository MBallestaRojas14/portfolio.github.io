Recipe #1

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 80g (for person)
Tomatoe 100g
Tuna 1 can
Basil One leaf

Step 1: Boil a large pot of salted water.

Step 2: Add the spaghetti and cook until done.

Step 3: Drain the spaghetti and heat the sauce of your choice in a skillet.

Step 4: Mix the drained spaghetti with the hot sauce.

Step 5: Serve hot and enjoy.

Recipe #2

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 1 piece of bread for each person
Meat 1 piece of hamburger for person
Bacon 2 Slices for person
Cheese 1 Slice for person

Step 1: Place the pre-made burgers on the grill or skillet.

Step 2: Cook until almost done.

Step 3: Add bacon on top of each burger and cover with a slice of cheese to melt.

Step 4: Cover the skillet or grill to help melt the cheese.

Step 5: Serve the burgers between toasted buns and enjoy the flavor combination.

Recipe #3

  • Time: 40 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Potatoes 1kg
Brave sauce 100g
Garlic 1/4 of garlic
Parsley 1 leaf

Step 1: Cut the potatoes into small pieces.

Step 2: Fry the potatoes until are golden brown.

Step 3: Prepare a spicy sauce with tomato, garlic, paprika and olive oil.

Step 4: Mix the sauce with the potatoes.

Step 5: Enjoy you meal.
